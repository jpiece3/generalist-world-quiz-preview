A/B Testing Recommendations

Test 1: Identity vs. Diagnostic Hook

A: "You're not scattered. You're a generalist." (identity)
B: "What's holding your generalist career back?" (diagnostic)
Metric: CTR and quiz start rate
Hypothesis: Identity hook wins for cold audiences; diagnostic wins for warm audiences who already know the term.

Test 2: Social Proof Placement

A: "47,000+ generalists trust us" in ad body
B: Community member testimonial quote
Metric: Quiz completion rate
Hypothesis: Numeric proof wins for Google (trust signal); testimonial wins for Meta (emotional signal).

Test 3: CTA Copy

A: "Take the Free Quiz"
B: "Find My Career Blocker"
C: "Get My Career Map"
Metric: Click-through rate
Hypothesis: Outcome-oriented CTAs (B, C) outperform generic "take quiz."

Test 4: Video vs. Static (Meta)

A: Milly founder story video (30s)
B: Bold text static image with brand colors
Metric: Cost per quiz completion
Hypothesis: Video wins for cold audiences (trust-building); static wins for retargeted (they already know the brand).

Test 5: Landing Page vs. Direct Quiz

A: Ad links to landing page (full context)
B: Ad links directly to quiz Q1 (skip landing)
Metric: Email capture rate
Hypothesis: Direct quiz entry wins for warm audiences; landing page wins for cold audiences who need context.

Test 6: Audience Segments (LinkedIn)

A: Job title targeting (Chief of Staff, PM, etc.)
B: Interest targeting (Career Development)
C: Lookalike of community members
Metric: Hot lead percentage from quiz
Hypothesis: Job title targeting yields highest-quality leads; interest targeting yields volume.
