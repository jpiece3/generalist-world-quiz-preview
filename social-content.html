30-Day Social Content Calendar

Content pillars, platform strategy, and a week-by-week calendar to drive quiz traffic and community engagement for Generalist World.

Content Pillars

Five pillars that map to the keyword clusters and quiz funnel messaging. Every post should fit at least one pillar.

🎯

Identity Validation

"You're not scattered" messaging. Normalises the generalist experience.

🧩

Quiz Promotion

Direct quiz CTAs, result teasers, profile reveals, and completion celebration.

🤝

Community Stories

Member testimonials, transformation stories, and behind-the-scenes community moments.

📚

Education & Frameworks

Career frameworks, skill-stacking models, and positioning tools -- value-first content.

🌐

Social Proof & Trends

Stats, research data, AI-era positioning, and "generalist vs specialist" discourse.

Platform Strategy

LinkedIn

Role

Primary B2B-adjacent channel. Career professionals, HR leaders, fractional executives.

Content types

  • Long-form text posts with hooks
  • Carousel (quiz flow previews)
  • Polls ("What's your biggest career blocker?")
  • Company page + Milly personal posts

Frequency

4-5 posts/week (mix of company and personal)

Best times

Tue-Thu 8-10am GMT, Tue/Wed 5-6pm GMT

Instagram

Role

Community building + brand warmth. Visual identity, behind-the-scenes, community celebration.

Content types

  • Reels (15-30s: quiz teasers, identity reframes)
  • Carousels (frameworks, tips, quiz walkthrough)
  • Stories (polls, Q&A, quiz link)
  • Feed posts (quotes, testimonials)

Frequency

3-4 feed posts/week + daily stories

Best times

Mon-Fri 11am-1pm GMT, Sat 10am GMT

X (Twitter)

Role

Thought leadership, discourse engagement, quick-hit frameworks. "Generalist vs specialist" conversations.

Content types

  • Threads (3-8 tweets: frameworks, quiz rationale)
  • Quote tweets (reframing specialist discourse)
  • Single tweets (stats, hot takes, quiz CTAs)
  • Replies to career/AI discourse

Frequency

5-7 tweets/week + 2-3 threads/month

Best times

Mon-Fri 12-2pm GMT, 5-7pm GMT
