Quiz Funnel Walkthrough

What's Holding Your
Generalist Career Back?

A diagnostic quiz funnel for Generalist World -- built to turn curious visitors into engaged community members through identity-affirming career insights.

Section 1

What This Is

This is a 7-question diagnostic quiz funnel that identifies the #1 thing holding someone's generalist career back -- and recommends the right Generalist World resource to fix it.

How it complements the existing quiz: The Generalist Archetype Quiz (39K+ completions) answers "What kind of generalist am I?" This Career Blocker Quiz answers "What's stopping me from thriving as one?" The two quizzes work as a pair -- identity + diagnosis.

Quiz Format

7 questions using 7 different interactive types (cards, slider, tags, emoji, radio, toggle, stars). Takes about 3 minutes.

Result Profiles

5 career profiles (Positioning Gap, Strategy Gap, Belonging Gap, Focus Gap, Renaissance Builder) each with personalised insights and recommendations.

Email Sequences

14 emails across 5 sequences (Welcome, Cold, Warm, Hot, Win-Back). All in Milly's voice. Temperature-segmented for relevance.

Analytics Dashboard

Admin dashboard at /admin showing funnel metrics, completion rates, temperature distribution, answer analysis, and UTM sources.

Section 2

How It Works

The user journey from first click to engaged subscriber, step by step.

1

Landing Page

Visitor arrives from social media, newsletter, or ads. They see a warm, identity-affirming headline ("What's Holding Your Generalist Career Back?") and a clear CTA to start. Social proof (47,000+ subscribers, 570+ community) builds trust.

2

7-Question Quiz

Each question uses a different interactive type for engagement. Questions progress from safe/cognitive (Q1-3) to emotional/diagnostic (Q4-5) to commitment/aspiration (Q6-7). Progress bar with encouraging messages keeps them moving.

3

Email Capture

After all 7 questions, they see: "Your career map is ready." They enter their email and name to see results. Value-focused copy explains what they'll get (strengths radar, profile, next steps). Skip option available.

4

Loading Screen

4 branded messages rotate while results calculate: "Mapping your generalist superpowers..." / "Identifying your career blocker..." / "Building your personalised strengths radar..." / "Matching you with your generalist profile..."

5

Results Page

Personalised results: 5-axis radar chart, profile name and headline, key insight, hidden insight, 3 actionable tips, and temperature-appropriate CTAs. Value FIRST, CTAs second.

6

Email Nurture

Automated sequences trigger based on score: Welcome (all), then Cold (0-49), Warm (50-79), or Hot (80-100). Win-back sequence activates after 14 days of no opens. All in Milly's voice.

Section 3

Why It Works

The strategy behind the quiz is grounded in research on Generalist World's audience and market position.

Proven Quiz Model

The existing Archetype Quiz has generated 39,000+ completions and 22,000+ newsletter subscribers. This new quiz builds on that proven model with a diagnostic angle.

Identity-First Psychology

Generalists have spent years feeling "broken." The quiz validates their identity before recommending anything. This builds deep trust and brand loyalty.

Temperature Segmentation

Instead of one-size-fits-all follow-up, every lead gets content matched to their readiness. Cold leads get education. Warm leads get frameworks. Hot leads get community.

AI-Era Relevance

The quiz positions generalists as uniquely adapted for the AI era -- cross-functional, integrative, creative. This hooks into active career anxiety and reframes it as an advantage.

Complementary to Existing Quiz

The Career Blocker Quiz feeds into the Archetype Quiz (and vice versa). Together they create a "full picture" that increases engagement and time-on-site.

Value-First Results

Results include a radar chart, profile insight, hidden insight, and 3 actionable tips -- all free. CTAs feel like natural next steps, not sales pitches.
